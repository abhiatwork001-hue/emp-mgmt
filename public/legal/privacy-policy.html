Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-01-14

1. Introduction

LaGasy ("we", "our", "us") provides a SaaS platform for HR, scheduling and operations management. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our clients in Portugal.

2. Data Controller and Processor

For each client (restaurant), the client acts as the Data Controller. LaGasy acts as the Data Processor and processes data only on the client’s instructions.

3. Personal Data Processed

  • Employee names, contact information, identification numbers (e.g., NIF)
  • Schedules, absences, vacation requests
  • Uploaded documents (contracts, certificates)
  • Messages exchanged within the platform
  • Audit logs of actions performed in the system

4. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed to enable the core functionalities of the platform: workforce scheduling, absence management, document storage, internal communication, and auditability.

5. Legal Basis

Processing is based on the performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and legitimate interests of the client and LaGasy (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

6. Data Retention

Employee personal data is retained for the period defined by the client, but not longer than the statutory limits. Audit logs are retained for 24 months. Upon contract termination, personal data will be deleted within 30‑90 days unless otherwise required by law.

7. Data Subject Rights

Users can exercise their rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability and objection via the platform’s Settings page.

8. Security Measures

All data is stored in an encrypted MongoDB instance, access is protected by HTTPS, passwords are hashed with bcrypt, and environment secrets are kept out of source control.

9. International Transfers

Data is hosted within the EU. No transfers outside the European Economic Area occur.

10. Contact

For privacy‑related inquiries, please contact privacy@lagasy.com.
