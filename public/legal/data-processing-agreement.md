# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Between**

- **Client (Restaurant)** – Data Controller
- **LaGasy** – Data Processor

## 1. Scope
LaGasy processes personal data on behalf of the Client solely for the purposes defined in the Service Agreement (HR, scheduling, document management, messaging, audit logs).

## 2. Obligations of the Data Processor
- Process data only on documented instructions from the Client.
- Implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures (encryption at rest, TLS in transit, access controls, regular backups).
- Assist the Client in responding to data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability).
- Notify the Client without undue delay of any personal data breach.
- Delete or return all personal data on termination of the contract, within 30‑90 days.

## 3. Obligations of the Data Controller
- Provide clear instructions on data processing activities.
- Ensure lawful basis for processing and obtain any necessary consents.
- Define retention periods for employee data.

## 4. Sub‑processing
LaGasy may engage sub‑processors (cloud hosting, email service) only with prior written consent of the Client and will ensure they provide equivalent data protection guarantees.

## 5. International Transfers
All data is stored within the EU. No transfers outside the European Economic Area occur.

## 6. Termination
Upon termination, LaGasy shall delete or return all personal data within 30‑90 days, unless retention is required by law.

## 7. Governing Law
This DPA is governed by the laws of Portugal.

*Signed by the authorized representatives of the Client and LaGasy.*
